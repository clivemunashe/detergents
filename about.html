About Us

GemGuard was founded by two young enthusiastic individuals in 2012 with experience in both manufacturing and management, in 2016 it was incorporated. Although it started with a modest amount of capital, today GemGuard is growing fast in every aspect in order to meet the demand of the fast growing industry. We are supported by a strong management team comprising of people with relevant technical experience. We manufacture a wide range of products high quality cleaning products, synthetic detergents, industrial surfacant, packing materials and disinfections aimed to meet the demand for he local market as well as international market.



What we do

We at GemGuard we believe that a good health is conducive to the productivity and quality of our surrounding. We strieve to provide quality detergents and excellent cleaning services to satisfy the challenging needs of our customers. In every aspect of our business we strieve to earn and keep the trust that our clients give us when they choose GemGuard products and services. As a company we are very interested in meeting the client/customer expectation whether the job is small or big.
